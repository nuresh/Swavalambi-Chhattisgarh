Message from the HVC
Prof. Alok Kumar Chakrawal

Vice Chancellor

Welcome to our Centre for Distance and Online Education. We are committed to providing world-class education that is accessible to all. Our programs are designed to empower you with the knowledge and skills needed for success in today's rapidly evolving world.

As the Vice Chancellor, I firmly believe that education is the key to personal and societal transformation. In a world of rapidly advancing technologies and shifting paradigms, our institution stands firm in its commitment to ensuring quality education that transcends geographical and economic barriers. We aim to bring you a holistic learning experience that goes beyond the confines of a traditional classroom.

Whether you are starting your academic journey or enhancing your professional expertise, our distance and online programs will equip you with the skills and knowledge you need to thrive in an ever-evolving world. We have carefully crafted our curriculum to align with industry standards, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the global workforce.

I invite you to take full advantage of the resources and opportunities available at the Centre for Distance and Online Education and wish you all the best in your educational pursuits.

Message from the Registrar
Prof. A.S. Ranadive

Registrar

Welcome to our vibrant learning community. As the Registrar, I am here to ensure your administrative journey is as smooth as possible. From the moment you enroll until you graduate, the Registrar's office is dedicated to supporting you every step of the way.

Whether you need assistance with enrollment, transcripts, or any other administrative matters, our team is here to help. We understand that managing your academic career can sometimes be challenging, and we are committed to providing you with the resources and support needed to make the process as seamless as possible.

We encourage you to reach out to the Registrar's office with any questions or concerns you may have. We look forward to helping you navigate your academic path with confidence and success.
